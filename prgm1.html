About Us

Juice is a drink made from the extraction or pressing of the natural liquid contained in fruit. It can also refer to liquids that are flavored with concentrate,such as clam juice. Juice is commonly consumed as a beverage or flavoring in foods or other beverages,such as smoothies. Juice emerged as a popular beverage choice after the development of pasteurization. Juice helps to maintain a good health. Juicing can play a role in a balanced diet. For instance, it can help you get fruits if you don't enjoy eating them. Juicing also can give the digestive system a rest from digesting fiber.
